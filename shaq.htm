Shaquille Rashaun O'Neal, nicknamed "Shaq", is an American retired professional basketball player who is a sports analyst on the television program Inside the NBA on TNT. He is widely considered one of the greatest players in the history of the NBA. At 7 ft 1 in tall and 325 pounds, he was one of the tallest and heaviest players in the history of the NBA. O'Neal played for six teams throughout his 19-year career.
